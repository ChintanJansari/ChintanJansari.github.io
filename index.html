Profile

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at TU Delft with over 8 years of experience in physics-based simulation and computational geometry.

My work bridges the gap between theoretical mechanics and advanced manufacturing. I develop algorithms that integrate manufacturing constraints into the optimization process, leveraging the design freedom of additive manufacturing to create eco-efficient, high-performance structures.
